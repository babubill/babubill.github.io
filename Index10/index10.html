The Egypt Trav-E-Log is presented in four geographic areas:

  • Cairo Region, which includes Giza, Dahshur, Saqqara, and Memphis.
  • Luxor Region, which includes Valley of the Kings, and Temples of Luxor, Habu, Karnak, and Al-Deir-Bahari.
  • Red Sea, which includes the beach town of Marsa Alam, and a fun item called 'Colors and Shapes'.
  • Aswan Region, which includes the famous dam on the Nile, Abu Simbel, Elephantinels, Philae, and the west bank.
