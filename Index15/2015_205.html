Dec 26, 2015, George Town, Penang Island, Malaysia
-------------------------------------------
Walked most of George Town's Core and Buffer Zones with a map in one hand, camera in t'other, taking pictures of street art. At times cars were parked so close to the display that photography was impossible. At other times, the sun cast a strong shadow of the display on the building, leading to a confusing double image. Then in the most popular spots, it was hard to get a clear shot because of all the people. It IS good to see so many folks appreciating all the work the artists and the city put into this project.
