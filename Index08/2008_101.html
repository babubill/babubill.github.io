Dar Es Salaam Arrival

back: 08 index page                           Arrival in DAR  

14 Sept 2008, Dar Es Salaam, Tanzania
-------------------------------------------
Thirty two Peace Corps volunteers arrived in Dar Es Salaam after a very long journey. From Philadelphia we traveled by bus to JFK, caught a flight to Amsterdam for a long layover, then finally boarded a second flight to complete the journey after a brief, late evening stop in Kilimanjaro. We were treated like VIPs at the DAR arrival desk; all formalities had been taken care of. It was late, so the staff wisked us away to our rooms for a welcome night's rest.
