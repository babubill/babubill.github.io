Flores Revisited

Hired an ojek so I could take more photos of bamboo designs along the highway in western Flores.

These photos were taken along the road from Labuan Bajo to Ruten. This area seems to have an abundance of painted panels. Some painters did not just enhance the woven design, but took artestic licence to create an entire new look.
