Bamboo Homes in Bagan

These photos are taken in the villages near the ancient town of Bagan.


Two building material stores are visited in Nyang U. Simple panels are being made in the middle of the driveway, so I inquire about the various patterns. The explanation is that each shop specializes in one or two patterns. Neither of these stores carry the diamond patterns; they direct me to another town which carries a different selection of patterns.
